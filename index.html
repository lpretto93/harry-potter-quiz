We are pleased to inform you that you have been accepted to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. As a student at Hogwarts, you must possess certain qualities in order to be worthy of admission. Among these are the courage to face the challenges ahead, the wisdom to make the right choices when confronted with difficult decisions, the creativity to approach magic with an open mind, compassion for your fellow students, and the determination to persevere in the face of adversity. These are the qualities we value and that we will nurture throughout your years here. Your potential for greatness is enormous, but it will be up to you to harness it and grow. In the coming days, you will be introduced to the magical arts, and you will discover your own path among the four Houses of Hogwarts: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin. Each House values different strengths, and you will find that it is not just your magic, but your heart and your spirit that will shape your journey.

We look forward to seeing how these qualities will shape your time here at Hogwarts. It will be a time of great joy, immense learning, and lasting friendships.

Yours sincerely,

Albus Dumbledore

Dear , Choose a House.

Gryffindor

Known for their bravery, courage, and daring, Gryffindors are a proud group of students who value determination and standing up for what is right. The house was founded by Godric Gryffindor and its emblematic animal is the lion.

Hufflepuff

Hufflepuff is known for its values of loyalty, hard work, and fair play. Students of Hufflepuff are known for their patience and kindness, and the house was founded by Helga Hufflepuff. The emblem animal is the badger.

Ravenclaw

Ravenclaw students are known for their intelligence, wisdom, and individuality. Founded by Rowena Ravenclaw, this house values creativity, learning, and open-mindedness. Its animal is the eagle.

Slytherin

Known for ambition, cunning, and resourcefulness, Slytherins are determined and strategic individuals. Founded by Salazar Slytherin, its emblem animal is the serpent. Slytherins value leadership and self-preservation.
